Our Features

Exceptional Language class

Our language class goes beyond traditional teaching methods, offering a dynamic and immersive learning experience that not only imparts linguistic skills but also fosters cultural understanding and practical application.

Customized training Programs

Developing customized language training programs for individuals based on their professional roles. Incorporating industry-specific vocabulary and communication skills into the language courses.

Highly Qualified Instructors

Our teaching staff is highly qualified with at least 10 years in the field, and are proficient in japanese and teaching it to non native speakers.

Visa And Immigration Assistance

Guiding individuals through the visa application process. Providing information on the specific visa requirements for various purposes (work, study, etc.). Assisting with necessary documentation for visa applications.

Legal And Regulatory Guidance

Compliance with Japanese laws and regulations is a critical aspect of doing anything abroad. Sachi Foundation provide legal advice and assistance in understanding and adhering to the regulatory landscape in Japan.

Feedback and Progress Monitoring

Offering regular feedback sessions and progress monitoring to ensure individuals are on track with their language learning goals. Adjusting the curriculum based on individual learning needs and feedback.
